E-Pay

Saia is now offering E-pay. E-pay (Electronic Invoice Payment) allows customers to selectively pay open invoices on-line. E-pay is easy and convenient to use and eliminates the cost involved in generating and mailing hard copy checks. To register for E-pay click on the Register button below. When registering for E-pay you must provide a default bank routing number and account number.

Not sure if E-pay is for you? Take a look at the E-pay FAQs.
