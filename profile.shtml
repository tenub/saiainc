Company Profile

We take service to the Xtreme.

Saia, Inc. (NASDAQ: SAIA) is among the leading regional less-than-truckload (LTL) companies in the United States with revenue exceeding $900 million annually, serving the Midwest, South, Southwest, Pacific Northwest, and Western regions of the United States. North American partner coverage is offered in Alaska, Hawaii, and New England and also extends to Canada, Mexico and Puerto Rico.
